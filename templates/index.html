Instructions:

Use the arrows to sort the grid of numbered tiles. Each move will shift an entire row or column. Every move shifts a tile outside of the puzzle grid and it reappears on the other side of the corresponding row or column. The game is won when the grid is sorted in ascending order from left to right and top to bottom. Hints are available for puzzles of size 3x3 or smaller.
